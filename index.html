About Me

I'm Joe Campbell, a junior at Tufts University in the School of Engineering. I expect to graduate in May 2018 with a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science and a Bachelor of Arts in Economics.

Simply put, I have a passion for creating useful, fun, and interesting applications by combining my passions for software engineering and business. Lately, I have become especially interested in the startup space and am actively working with startups on their products.

I am driven by the possibility of creating something great and hope to accomplish that one day.
